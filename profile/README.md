# UPTAC KomSai Projects

A collection of student projects, coursework outputs, experiments, and research prototypes related to Computer Science courses.

This organization serves as a shared repository space for projects developed by students under Computer Science classes and mentorship at the University of the Philippines Tacloban College.

---

## Focus Areas

Projects may include:

- Web Development
- Mobile Applications
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Data Science
- Database Systems
- Software Engineering
- Networking and Cybersecurity
- Embedded Systems and IoT
- Research and Thesis Projects

---

## Purpose

This organization aims to:

- Archive and organize student projects
- Encourage collaborative software development
- Promote version control and documentation practices
- Showcase practical applications of Computer Science concepts
- Provide references for future students

---

## Repository Structure

Repositories are generally organized by:

- Course
- Academic Year
- Project Title
- Student Group

Example:

```text
cmsc127-library-system
cmsc191-flood-prediction
capstone-smart-farming
```

---

## Notes

Most repositories are academic projects and may still be under development, experimental, or incomplete.

The content and implementations inside each repository belong to their respective student authors and contributors.
